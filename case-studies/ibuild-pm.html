iBuild PM — personnel supply & project delivery

A full website for an industrial delivery business operating across Australia, APAC, and the Middle East — with three integrated portals on one platform.

ibuildpm.com.au →

$19B+project value supported
450+peak team mobilised
3portals on one site

The challenge

iBuild PM needed a site that could win industrial contracts, recruit field personnel, and communicate an AIM investor narrative — without three separate products duct-taped together. The business operates in high-compliance environments where credibility and clarity matter.

What we built

  • Marketing site — capability statement, delivery model, sector coverage, and enquiry capture
  • Careers board — database-backed job listings with formatted descriptions and applicant submissions
  • AIM investor area — investor timeline, metrics, and register form under appropriate disclaimers
  • Staff portal — applicant tracking, onboarding, job management, timesheets, and admin workspace

Outcome

A single cohesive platform that supports sales, recruitment, investor relations, and internal operations — built on a real database backend rather than static pages or disconnected tools.
