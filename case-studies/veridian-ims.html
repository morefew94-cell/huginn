Veridian IMS — ISO compliance software

The challenge

Veridian IMS sells complex compliance software to construction, fabrication, mining, and engineering teams. The site needed to explain multiple product tiers, support annual/monthly pricing, handle free trial signups, and convert visitors without a sales call — all while maintaining trust in a high-compliance market.

What we built

  • Product positioning — sector pages, certification stages, and platform overview
  • Tiered pricing — six plans from standalone ISO modules to full IMS Complete
  • Stripe checkout — self-serve subscription purchase flow
  • Free trial signup — 7-day trial with no credit card required
  • Feature breakdowns — register lists, workflow tools, and comparison tables
  • Lead magnets — ISO implementation checklist and readiness content

Outcome

A SaaS marketing site that supports both self-serve revenue and sales-assisted demos — with the technical depth to grow as the product adds features and tiers.
