Butterfly Beauty — local studio, online presence

A tier 1 Starter website for a beauty studio — mobile-first design, service pages, and enquiry capture. Domain, hosting, and going live included. The client owns the site outright.

5pages
20business days
100%client-owned

The brief

Butterfly Beauty needed a simple, elegant site that reflected the studio's brand and made it easy for local clients to enquire about services. No complex integrations — just a professional online presence that works on mobile.

What we built

  • Homepage — brand positioning, hero messaging, and clear call to action
  • Service pages — treatments and offerings with enquiry paths
  • Contact & enquiry capture — forms wired for lead notifications
  • Domain & launch — domain registered, site hosted, and live on the internet

Package & timeline

Built on the Starter (tier 1) package — approximately 20 business days from deposit to launch: 10 days for a working draft, then 10 days for unlimited revisions. After final sign-off, the client owns the site. Future changes require a care plan or a one-off $99 change window (10 business days).
