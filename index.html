FAQ

Common questions

Straight answers on scope, pricing, timelines, and who we work with — Australia, the US, and remote clients worldwide.

What types of businesses does Huginnn work with?

Service businesses, SaaS and software companies, e-commerce brands, and professional services firms that need a custom site — not a template — with lead capture, SEO, and room to grow.

Do you work with clients in Australia and the United States?

Yes. Huginnn is remote-first and works with clients across Australia and the United States. AUD pricing is available for Australian clients.

How much does a custom website cost?

Build packages start from $1,500–$2,500 for Starter (up to 5 pages), $3,000–$6,000 for Growth (custom design and CMS), and $8,000–$15,000 for Pro bespoke builds. Monthly care plans run from $99–$1,499 depending on scope.

What is included in a website care plan?

Essentials covers hosting, security updates, daily backups, and uptime monitoring. Growth adds monthly content updates, SEO reporting, and analytics review. Partner includes ongoing optimisation, lead-generation support, and priority changes.

How long does a website build take?

Every project follows survey, plan, build, and handover. Timeline depends on package and scope — Starter sites are typically faster; Pro builds with custom functionality take longer. You receive a site plan with pages, cost, and timeline before design starts.

Do you build e-commerce and SaaS websites?

Yes. Growth packages support booking, quote, and signup flows. Pro packages include bespoke Next.js builds for e-commerce, SaaS products, membership sites, customer portals, and integrations with payments, CRM, and third-party tools.
