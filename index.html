FAQ

Common questions

Straight answers on scope, pricing, timelines, and who we work with — Australia, the US, and remote clients worldwide.

What is included in the Starter (tier 1) website?

Up to 5 pages, mobile-first design, lead capture forms, and search console / local SEO setup. The one-off fee includes domain registration (first year), hosting setup, and getting your site live on the internet. Hosting can be through Huginn or handed over if you prefer to self-host. Timeline is approximately 20 business days — 10 days for a working draft, then 10 days for unlimited revisions.

Do I own my website?

Yes. You own the site outright once the build is complete and paid in full. Huginn does not retain ownership. After finalisation, changes aren't included in the build fee — add a care plan for ongoing updates, or pay a one-off $99 change window (10 business days) for a short round of edits.

When do I need a care plan?

If you plan to update your site regularly after launch. The one-off build fee covers domain, hosting setup, and going live — not ongoing edits. If you only need changes once in a while, a one-off $99 change window (10 business days) is available without subscribing to a care plan.

What happens after my site is finalised?

Once you sign off on the final version, the build phase is complete and no further changes are included. Need edits later? Either purchase a care plan for ongoing updates, or a one-off $99 change window — 10 business days to request and approve revisions.

How do deposits work?

Each build package has a deposit to secure your slot: $20 for Starter (limited special), $150 for Growth (5% of $2,999), and $500 for Pro (5% of $9,999). Deposits are paid via Stripe checkout. The remaining balance is due before launch.

What types of businesses does Huginn work with?

Service businesses, SaaS and software companies, e-commerce brands, and professional services firms that need a custom site — not a template — with lead capture, SEO, and room to grow.

Do you work with clients in Australia and the United States?

Yes. Huginn is remote-first and works with clients across Australia and the United States. AUD pricing is available for Australian clients.

How much does a custom website cost?

Build packages start from $20 for Starter during our limited special (domain + going live — regular $499), $2,999 for Growth (custom design and CMS), and $9,999 for Pro bespoke builds. Care plans run from $99–$1,249/month if you need ongoing updates, with 6- and 12-month subscriptions available (12 months save 20%).

What is included in a website care plan?

Every care plan includes hosting with Huginn — your site is hosted and managed on our infrastructure at huginnweb.com. Essentials adds security updates, daily backups, and uptime monitoring. Growth adds monthly content updates, SEO reporting, and performance review. Partner includes ongoing optimisation, lead-generation support, and priority changes. Not ready for a subscription? A one-off $99 change window (10 business days) covers occasional post-launch edits without a care plan.

How long does a website build take?

Starter (tier 1) builds take approximately 20 business days — 10 days for a working draft, then 10 days for unlimited revisions. Growth and Pro timelines depend on scope; you receive a site plan with pages, cost, and timeline before design starts.

Do you set up Google Analytics?

No. Analytics setup is not included in build packages — that's your responsibility if you want tracking. We do set up search console and on-page SEO so your site can be found.

Can I add extras like logo design or copywriting?

Yes. Extras are optional add-ons for any package — logo design (from $99), brand kit (from $599), copywriting (from $99/page), extra pages, stock photography, business email, Google Business Profile setup, and more. We add them to your build invoice when we quote the project — not as separate cart items at checkout.

Can I make changes without a care plan?

Yes. If your site is already finalised and you haven't purchased a care plan, a one-off $99 change window covers revisions for 10 business days. For regular or ongoing updates, a monthly care plan is the better fit.

Do you build e-commerce and SaaS websites?

Yes. Growth packages support booking, quote, and signup flows. Pro packages include bespoke Next.js builds for e-commerce, SaaS products, membership sites, customer portals, and integrations with payments, CRM, and third-party tools.
