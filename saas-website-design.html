What SaaS sites need (that templates miss)

A SaaS website isn't a brochure — it's part of your sales funnel. It needs to explain a complex product quickly, handle pricing logic, capture product-qualified leads, and often connect to billing, CRM, or a product demo. Huginn builds these flows properly, not as afterthoughts.
